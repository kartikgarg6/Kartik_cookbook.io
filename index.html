Welcome To Kartik's Cookbook

My Story

Cooking started as a hobby for me in my early childhood when my nani taught me how to make maggi. I was so fascinated by cooking that i soon started paying attention in the kitchen. Me and my Dadu started cooking Sunday brunch together. In the lockdown of 2020, i started cooking more extensively. I helped my mother in the kitchen and learned so many new recipes.

This is a small recipe book i curated for all the food lovers like you, i hope you enjoy it!

Chef Kartik
